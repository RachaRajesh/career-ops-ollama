What does this actually do?

Three things that used to take me hours every day.

💯

Score jobs instantly

Paste a job URL and get a 1-5 score based on how well it matches your resume. No more reading 200-word descriptions just to find out you're not qualified.

📄

Tailor your resume

For each job, it rewords your existing bullets to surface the keywords they're looking for. No lying, no inflating — just better framing of what you've already done.

🕵️

Auto-fill applications

Opens the actual application form in a browser, fills in your details, and pauses for you to review. You still click submit — it just saves you the tedious typing.

Why does "local" matter?

Your job search is nobody else's business.

🔒

Your resume stays on your laptop

No cloud servers, no "we store your data securely" promises. It's just files on your machine.

🌐

Works offline

Once the model is downloaded, you don't need internet for the AI part. Works on a plane, in a cafe, anywhere.

💰

No API costs

No per-request charges. Download the model once, use it forever. Your electricity bill won't even notice.

🛡️

No rate limits

Evaluate 10 jobs or 1000. No "you've exceeded your quota" messages. It's your hardware.

Who is this for?

Honestly? Anyone who's tired of the job search grind.

  • New grads applying to 50+ jobs a week and getting burned out
  • Career switchers who need help framing old experience for new roles
  • Anyone on a visa who needs tailored resumes but can't afford a service
  • People who value privacy and don't want their resume on some startup's server

Get it running

Takes about 15 minutes. Most of that is downloading the model.

1

Install Ollama

It's the thing that runs AI models on your computer. brew install ollama on Mac, or grab it from ollama.com for Windows/Linux.

2

Download a model

This is the big download — 5-20 GB. Only happens once. ollama pull qwen2.5:14b is the sweet spot for most laptops.

3

Clone and install

git clone https://github.com/RachaRajesh/career-ops-ollama.git
cd career-ops-ollama && npm install

4

Add your resume

Copy cv.example.md to cv.md and paste your real resume in. This is what the AI compares jobs against.

5

Run it

npm start — you'll see a menu. Pick option 1 to evaluate your first job. That's it.
