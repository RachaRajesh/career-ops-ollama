Title: Senior Applied AI Engineer
Company: Acme Labs
Location: Remote (US) or San Francisco, CA

About the role

We're hiring a Senior Applied AI Engineer to lead the design and rollout of
our next-generation retrieval + agentic stack. You'll own the full lifecycle:
data pipelines, retrieval algorithms, eval harnesses, and production
deployment. You'll work closely with our research team to productionize
frontier techniques.

What you'll do
- Design and ship retrieval systems over heterogeneous data (docs, code,
  telemetry, structured records)
- Build evaluation frameworks for LLM-powered features and drive eval-guided
  development across the company
- Integrate agentic workflows (tool use, planning, reflection) into
  production systems serving 5M+ users
- Mentor mid-level engineers and set technical direction for the applied AI org

Requirements
- 4+ years building production ML / AI systems
- Deep experience with LLMs (prompt engineering, fine-tuning, RAG)
- Strong Python; comfort with at least one of Go, Rust, or TypeScript
- Experience with vector databases and retrieval algorithms
- Published eval methodology or similar is a plus

Compensation: $180k–$240k base + equity + benefits. Remote OK in US.
Sponsorship available for existing visa holders (H-1B transfer, no cap-subject).
